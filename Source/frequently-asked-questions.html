Discover Endless Opportunities: Enroll Your Child at Lewisham After School Club

  • Home
  • FAQs

Our after-school programs cater to children of various age groups, typically ranging from primary school age to secondary school age. We offer age-appropriate activities and curriculum to ensure that all children have a positive and enriching experience at our club.

Our coding program offers a variety of activities designed to engage and inspire students. Depending on the level and interests of the students, activities may include learning coding languages such as Python and Scratch, creating games and animations, building websites, and exploring advanced programming concepts.

The safety and well-being of the children in our care are our top priorities. We have strict protocols in place to ensure a safe and secure environment, including background checks for all staff, regular safety inspections of our facilities, and adherence to all relevant health and safety guidelines.

Enrolling your child in our after-school programs is easy! Simply visit our website to learn more about our programs, including schedules, pricing, and enrollment procedures. You can also contact us directly via phone or email for personalized assistance with the enrollment process. We look forward to welcoming your child to Lewisham After School Club!

At Lewisham After School Club, we are passionate about creating a positive impact on the lives of young learners.
