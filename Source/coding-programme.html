Our Coding Programme

Overview

At Lewisham After School Club, we understand the importance of equipping students with the skills they need to thrive in the digital age. Our Coding Programme is designed to introduce students to the exciting world of computer programming in a fun and interactive environment.

Why Learn to Code?

In today's technology-driven world, coding skills are more valuable than ever. Whether students aspire to become software engineers, data scientists, or entrepreneurs, understanding the fundamentals of coding can open up a world of opportunities. From building websites and mobile apps to creating games and animations, coding empowers students to bring their ideas to life and solve real-world problems.

What We Offer

Our Coding Programme covers a wide range of coding languages, tools, and concepts, catering to students of all ages and skill levels.

  • Hands-On Learning: Our coding courses provide hands-on experience with a variety of programming languages, including Python, Scratch, HTML/CSS, and more. Through interactive projects and activities, students learn fundamental coding concepts and develop problem-solving skills that are essential for success in STEM fields.
  • Experienced Instructors: Our experienced instructors are passionate about teaching coding and inspiring the next generation of programmers. They provide personalized guidance and support to ensure that each student progresses at their own pace and feels confident in their coding abilities.
  • Diverse Curriculum: Our curriculum covers a wide range of coding languages and concepts, including Python, Scratch, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and more. Whether students are beginners or have some coding experience, our program offers something for everyone to learn and explore.
  • Creativity and Problem-Solving: is not just about writing lines of code; it's about solving problems and expressing creativity. Our program encourages students to think critically,
  • Community and Collaboration: Coding is often a collaborative endeavor. Our coding programme fosters a sense of community and collaboration among students, allowing them to learn from each other, share ideas, and collaborate on projects. Through teamwork and peer support, students develop valuable interpersonal skills that are essential for success in the digital world.

Coding Outcomes

  • Introduction to Technology
  • Introduction to Computers
  • Enhanced Logical Thinking
  • Improved Maths Skills
  • Boosted Creativity
  • Increased Confidence
